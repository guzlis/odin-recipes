Avgolemono

Description:

This classic greek chicken soup is perfect for cold winter evenings. Its egg-lemon sauce is guaranteed to give you an extra dose of vitamin C, while the rest of it will keep you warm and cozy. Traditionally, it is made from scratch by boiling a whole chicken, but for all of our time's sake, this version of the recipe is weekday evening friendly and should take around 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup finely carrots (chopped)
  • 1 cup celery (chopped)
  • 1 cup green onion (chopped)
  • 4+ garlic cloves (some sites say 2 suffice, but don't listen to them, they're liars!)
  • 8 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup of rice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cooked and shredded boneless chicken breast
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)
  • 1 large, heavy pot
  • 1 medium bowl to prepare the egg-lemon sauce in
  • 1 soup ladle

Steps:

  1. in the pot, add 1 tablespoon of olive oil and heat on medium high. Add the onions, celery and green onion and sauté for a brief moment
  2. Add the garlic, continue sauteing for a little longer
  3. Add the broth and bay leaves to the pot and heat up high
  4. Once it is boiling, add rice, salt and pepper, reduce heat to medium low, let it simmer for 20 minutes
  5. Once the rice is tender, add the chicken breast pieces
  6. In the bowl, whisk the eggs and lemon juice together
  7. While continuously whisking, add 2 soup ladles of the chicken broth
  8. Transfer the sauce from the bowl into the pot, stir together and immediately remove the pot from the stove

And there you have it! Enjoy your meal, or, as the greeks say, Καλή όρεξη! / kalí órexi
